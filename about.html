About Us

Neighborhood roots, contemporary craft

Founded by chef Mara Ilves, Harvest & Hearth began with one idea: make local produce feel celebratory and deeply comforting. Every week, we build around what nearby farms are harvesting and what guests are craving.

Inside, you'll find terracotta textures, olive accents, and soft lighting inspired by old-world dining rooms, interpreted with modern restraint.

We're proud to be a small team where service is personal, plates are generous, and every table feels like it's been saved just for you.
